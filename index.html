About me

Hello, I am Ziming Wang, a master of philosophy postgraduate student in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). I am dedicated to using my mathematical knowledge to explain and solve real-world problems, particularly those related to traffic scenarios. I have published in refereed journals, such as IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCASII, Nonlinear Dynamics and Cognitive Computation. I am presently supervised by Prof. Huan YU and co-supervised by Prof. Yiding JI.


I am actively looking for academic collaboration in 2026! Please feel free to contact me if you have any ideas! 😊
