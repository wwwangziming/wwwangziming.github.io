    About me

    Hi, welcome to my corner of the Internet!

    Hello, I am Ziming Wang, a master of philosophy postgraduate student in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). I am currently focusing my research on using mathematical knowledge to solve problems related to control theory. I have published in refereed journals, such as IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCASII, Nonlinear Dynamics and Cognitive Computation. I am presently supervised by Prof. Huan YU and co-supervised by Prof. Yiding JI.
